  5. CHURCH TO WRITE AN INTRODUCTORY LETTER CONCERNING THE COUPLE, THEIR MARIRAL STATUS (Bachelor, Spinister, Widow(er), Divorced person or Married under Customary law ) AND WEDDING DATE TO THE REGISTRAR OF MARRIAGES VOI. PLEASE NOTE: THE LETTER SHOULD HAVE A CHURCH STAMP OF THE VENUE OF THE WEDDING
  6. BLANK COPY OF MARRIAGE CERTIFICATE OF MARRIAGE DULLY STAMPED AT THE BACK AND A COPY OF SERIAL NUMBER OF THE MARRIAGE BOOK
  7. FOR A DIVORCED PERSON WHO WANTS TO REMARRY - A COPY OF DECREE OF ABSOLUTE (DIVORCE) OR NISI
  8. PERSONS MARRYING FOR THE FIRST TIME i.e (Bachelor OR Spinister) AND A COUPLE ALREADY LIVING TOGETHER i.e (MARRIED UNDER CUSTOMARY LAW) TO BRING A LETTER FROM AREA CHIEF OR ASSISTANT CHIEF CONFIRMING MARITAL STATUS AND AREA OF JURISIDICTION
  9. CONSENT LETTER FROM BRIDES PARENTS
  10. NOTICE OF MARRIAGE - KSHS 600 1ST DAY
  11. REGISTRAR CERTIFICATE - KSHS. 800 AFTER 21 DAYS
  12. WHEN PLACING THE NOTICE, BOTH THE BRIDE AND THE GROOM TO BE PRESENT
