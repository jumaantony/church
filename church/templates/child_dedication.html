Child Dedication

Behold, children are a gift of the Lord, The fruit of the womb is a reward. - Psalm 127:3

Child Dedication Procedure

  1. Plan a Parents' Meeting

    Through the church council, you can schedule a meeting with the pastor to discuss the dedication of your child. The pastor will explain the process and answer any questions you may have.

  2. Provide Parent Trainning

    Parental Trainning is arranged several days before the dedication date. This trainning is used to teach and explain the dedication service and reignforce a biblical vision for Christian parenting.

  3. Day of the Service - Affirmations

    The family will be invited to the fron of the church along wiwth their child where they will affirm their commitment to raise their child in the fear and admonition of the Lord.

  4. Day of Service - Blessing Prayer

    After the parents have affirmed their commitment, the Bishop will pray for the child and the parents.
