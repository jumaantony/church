Praise and Adoration

Let the word of Christ richly dwell within you, with all wisdom teaching and admonishing one another ...- Collosians 3:16

The biblical aspect of Praise and Adoration team is to lead the congregation during Worship. The team co-ordinates with Mission and Evangelism of the Church to ensure there is solemn devotion in all Church services.

Praise and Adoration Objectives

  • Increase diversity in worship experiences
  • Improve participation revival meetings in all revival meetings
  • Learn news ways and approaches
  • Nurture future talent for continuity
  • Set aside resources in enhancing our worship experience
  • To create a conducive atmosphere of Worship during church services/ fellowships
  • Plan and execute capacity building in worship
  • To lead people in worship, prayer and supplication in making their needs.
